About Arpeggiators

An arpeggiator breaks chords into individual notes played in sequence. This demo features 13 different patterns, from simple up/down arpeggios to complex converging and pinched patterns. Experiment with different chord types, octave ranges, and rhythmic divisions to create everything from classic synth sequences to modern electronic textures.

Pattern Guide:

  • Up/Down: Basic ascending/descending patterns
  • Up-Down/Down-Up: Bouncing patterns that reverse direction
  • Converge/Diverge: Alternating between outer and inner notes
  • Pinched: Start with extremes, then fill in the middle
  • Random/Shuffle: Unpredictable note orders
  • Chord: All notes played simultaneously
