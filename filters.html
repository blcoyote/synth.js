üìö Filter Guide

Lowpass: Classic synth filter. Higher resonance creates "squelch" effect.
Highpass: Remove bass/rumble. Good for thin, airy sounds.
Bandpass: Isolate frequency range. Great for vocal formants and wah effects.
Notch: Remove specific problem frequency. Phaser effects when swept.
Peaking: Surgical EQ. Boost presence or cut harshness.
Shelving: Broad bass/treble control. Natural-sounding tone shaping.
