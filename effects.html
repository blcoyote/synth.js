üéõÔ∏è About Dynamic Bus Routing

This demo showcases the dynamic bus system! Effects are added and removed from the audio bus in real-time without stopping the sound.

Try this: Start playing a sound, then add effects one by one. Change the order, remove effects, add them back. The signal chain is rebuilt instantly - this is the power of modular architecture!

Delay: Rhythmic echoes. Try 375ms (eighth note at 120 BPM).
Reverb: Adds space. Great on pads and leads.
Distortion: Adds harmonics and grit. Classic on bass and leads.
Chorus: Thickens the sound. Beautiful on clean tones.
